Welcome to Legends.


For over 2 decades, Legends has been serving League City and the SE Texas area. Many other pool halls have come and gone, but it is Legends that has withstood that test of time.

What do we have to offer?

Legends has built a reputation of excellence. We have twenty 8 ft. tables along with three 9 ft. tables, all felted with 860 Simonis Cloth. We are the home of the Houston 9-Ball Open, as well as a host for the Clear Lake 8 Ball League and BCA Pool League.

We do not aim at just the professional.

Legends has established a place with a family-friendly atmosphere. And why not? We have daily specials on our rental rates which are all times per hour per person, we only charge for up to two people. We have a happy hour every day from open until 7pm, plus additional drink specials on selected nights.

So, please come check us out.
